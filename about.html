About Netranjali Polyclinic

Netranjali polyclinic placed in the heart of the city at Rajapeth is giving advanced and affordable Eye and Dental Services through Dr Navin Soni(Ophthalmologist), Dr Manmohan Soni (Endodontist) and Dr Nishita Soni(Dental Surgeon) since few years.

With use for Modern technology, advanced equipments and Skill its a centre for excellence in eye surgery and dental surgery in Amravati. They are also catering to nearby talukas and districts for their superspecailization field by weekly/monthly Visits.

Our Team

Dr. Navin Soni
Consultant Ophthalmologist

Dr Navin Soni who stood merit in board exams in his school and Jr college in his home town Amravati went to GMC, Nagpur for his MBBS in 2001. In 2008 he got admission in prestigious Nair hospital Mumbai for M.S. Ophthalmology and passed the same with flying colors and topping theory exams in state, simultaneously he also cleared his DOMS, DNB and FICO(UK) exams around that period along with M.O.ship job in Municipal Eye Hospital at Byculla. With special interest in Cornea and Refractive laser procedures he went for his fellowship in same field at Ahmedabad followed by short term fellowship at Melbourne, Australia.

Currently, he is a cornea and cataract surgeon and consultant at Amravati, with visiting consultations at various places in Vidarbha like Nagpur, Akola, Washim, Khamgaon, Karanja, Morshi to name a few. He is currently doing various types of simple and complex Surgeries on eye at his hospital including no injection, no anesthesia, no patch techniques for cataract, lasik, pterygium surgeries. His center is also one of the few places in the vicinity with complex cornea Surgeries being done including Corneal Transplantation and Cross-linking procedures.

Dr. Manmohan Soni
CONSERVATIVE DENTIST AND ENDODONTIST

Dr Manmohan Soni obtained his 'Bachelor of Dental Surgery' degree from Sharad Pawar Dental College, Sawangi (Meghe), Wardha, India during years 2006-11 under the DMIMS University, Nagpur and did his Masters in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics from M S Ramaiah Dental College & Hospital, Banglore from 2012-15 from RGUHS, Bangalore, Karnataka.

Dr Manmohan Soni is practicing at his own private clinic since 2015. Also he is an consultant Endodontist at Amravati, Nanded and nearby rural areas for Single Visit RCT's and Restorative & Esthetic Dentistry. Dr Manmohan Soni is also well versed with micro dentistry i.e. use of Microscope in field of dentistry.

Dr. Nishita Soni
DENTAL SURGEON & COSMETIC DENTIST

Dr Nishita Soni completed her 'Bachelor of Dental Surgery' degree from the prestigious Government Dental College & Hospital, Hyderabad during 2011-16 from Dr NTR University of Health Sciences, Hyderabad and has been in practice ever since. She has a special interest in cosmetic dentistry which includes Esthetic fillings etc & is excellent with the treatment of little kids.

Her biggest strength is her constant eagerness to learn about advances in her subject of interest which translates into better Patient care.
